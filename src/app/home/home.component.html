Welcome to Scroll-io!

This is the ever-expanding catalog of Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition homebrew content from The Cobbler Barrel. Navigating using the categorical links above will take you to the indexed and searchable tables of various homebrew articles. The website gets frequent content and code updates, so always check back in to find something new. Feel free to browse, copy, and make your own!
